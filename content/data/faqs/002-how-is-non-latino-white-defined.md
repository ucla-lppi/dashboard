question: "How is non-Latino white defined?"
draft: false
---
“Non-Latino white” (NL white) refers to individuals who identify as white but not Hispanic or Latino. According to the U.S. Census Bureau and the American Community Survey (ACS), [white](https://www.census.gov/topics/population/race/about.html?utm_source) is defined as “a person having origins in any of the original peoples of Europe, the Middle East, or North Africa."
