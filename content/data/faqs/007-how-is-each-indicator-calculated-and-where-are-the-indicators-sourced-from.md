Each indicator is sourced from free, publicly available datasets. Common data sources include:

- The Census Bureau’s [American Community Survey (ACS)](https://www.census.gov/programs-surveys/acs) for demographic and socioeconomic data.
- The State of California's [CalEnviroScreen 4.0](https://oehha.ca.gov/calenviroscreen/report/calenviroscreen-40) for environmental indicators.
- The U.S. [Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC)](https://www.cdc.gov) for public health data.

See the [Our Data section](https://latinoclimatehealth.org/our-data) or our [technical report](https://latino.ucla.edu/research/climate-health-dashboard-technical-doc/) for more information on our methods and data sources.
