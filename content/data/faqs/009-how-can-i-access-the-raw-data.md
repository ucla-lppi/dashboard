The Latino Climate and Health Dashboard provides key insights through county factsheets, but users seeking raw data can access: 
- Our [GitHub repository](https://github.com/ucla-lppi/report-generator), where we share the datasets used in our analysis.
- Official data sources listed in the [technical report](https://latino.ucla.edu/research/climate-health-dashboard-technical-doc/).
