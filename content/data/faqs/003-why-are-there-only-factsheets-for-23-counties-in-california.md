question: "Why are there only factsheets for 23 counties in California?"
draft: false
---
We analyzed 23 counties that satisfied at least one of the following criteria: 

1) Number of Latino-majority census tracts. We ranked counties by the number of census tracts where Latinos make up more than 70% of the population. We included the 20 counties with the largest number of Latino-majority census tracts. 

2) Total Latino Population. We ranked counties by their total Latino population. We included the 20 counties with the highest Latino populations.

Combining the two lists results in 23 counties that represent 93% of California’s Latino population, providing comprehensive coverage of the state’s Latino population. 

Please refer to our [technical report](https://latino.ucla.edu/research/climate-health-dashboard-technical-doc/) for more information on how we selected the 23 counties included in the dashboard and for a full list of the counties.
