The average number of years an individual is expected to live from birth based on current mortality rates. Life expectancy is an age-adjusted indicator, ensuring fair comparisons across counties with differing age structures. Data for this indicator reflects a 3-year average (2019-2021) produced by County Health Rankings & Roadmaps. See more detailed information [online](https://www.countyhealthrankings.org/health-data/health-outcomes/length-of-life/life-expectancy?year=2024&county=06025).
