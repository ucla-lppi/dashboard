The average number of days in a calendar year when the daily temperature reached a minimum of 90°F. We averaged data across 5 years, from 2018 to 2022. See detailed information [online](https://ephtracking.cdc.gov), and for more information on how we constructed this indicator, please refer to our [technical report](https://latino.ucla.edu/research/climate-health-dashboard-technical-doc/).
