The average annual diesel particulate matter emissions in tons. Data are from 2016. Diesel PM is the particle phase of exhaust, commonly known as “soot,” and is produced by diesel engines commonly used to power trucks, buses, cars, ships, trains, and heavy-duty equipment. See more detailed information [online](https://oehha.ca.gov/media/downloads/calenviroscreen/report/calenviroscreen40reportf2021.pdf).
