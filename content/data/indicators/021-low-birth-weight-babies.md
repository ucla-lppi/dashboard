The average percentage of babies born weighing less than 5.5 pounds. LBW increases the risk of infant mortality, developmental delays, and chronic health conditions. Data on LBW reflect a 7-year average from 2009 to 2015. See more detailed information [online](https://oehha.ca.gov/media/downloads/calenviroscreen/report/calenviroscreen40reportf2021.pdf).
