The average percentage of adults over 18 classified as obese. Obesity is defined as having a body mass index (BMI) greater than or equal to 30.0 kg/m² and is calculated using self-reported weight and height. The 2023 release of PLACES reflects data from 2021. See more detailed information [online](https://www.cdc.gov/places/measure-definitions/health-outcomes.html).
