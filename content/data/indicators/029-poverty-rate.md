The number and average percentage of individuals living in poverty. The Census Bureau’s poverty threshold for a given year varies depending on the number of adults and children in a family and is adjusted annually for inflation. For example, the average poverty threshold for a family of four with two children under 18 was $29,678 in 2022. See more detailed information [online](https://www.census.gov/programs-surveys/acs/technical-documentation/code-lists.2022.html#list-tab-155790978).
