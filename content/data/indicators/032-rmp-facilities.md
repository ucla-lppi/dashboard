The average proximity score to RMP facilities. Proximity scores consider the number of facilities and their proximity to neighborhoods. Proximity scores range from 0 to 10, with higher scores representing closer proximity. Information on active RMP facilities is pulled and updated for each new iteration of the Environmental Protection Agency Environmental Justice Screening Tool from the RMP National Program System. See more detailed information [online](https://www.epa.gov/system/files/documents/2024-07/ejscreen-tech-doc-version-2-3.pdf).
