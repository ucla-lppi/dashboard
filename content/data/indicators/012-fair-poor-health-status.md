The percentage of individuals who report their health as “fair” or “poor”. Data for fair or poor health status is self-reported and reflects individuals’ perceptions of their overall health. For this indicator, we pooled data over the 2018-2022 period to align with the 5-year American Community Survey methodology. See more detailed information [online](https://healthpolicy.ucla.edu/our-work/california-health-interview-survey-chis).
