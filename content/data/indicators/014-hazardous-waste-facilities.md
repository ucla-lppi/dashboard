The average exposure score to hazardous waste facilities. Exposure scores consider the number of sites and facilities and their neighborhood proximity. Higher scores indicate greater exposure to hazardous waste facilities. Data on hazardous waste facilities are from 2018 to 2020. See more detailed information [online](https://oehha.ca.gov/media/downloads/calenviroscreen/report/calenviroscreen40reportf2021.pdf). Hazardous waste facilities include treatment, storage, and disposal sites, which can release toxic substances such as carcinogens, mercury, and asbestos into the air, water, and soil.
