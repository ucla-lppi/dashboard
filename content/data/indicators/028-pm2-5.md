The average annual concentration of PM2.5 in micrograms per cubic meter (µg/m³). This measure represents data averaged over 3 years, from 2015 to 2017. 
Particulate matter is a mixture of aerosolized solid and liquid particles, including organic chemicals, dust, allergens, and metals. See more detailed information [online](https://oehha.ca.gov/media/downloads/calenviroscreen/report/calenviroscreen40reportf2021.pdf).
