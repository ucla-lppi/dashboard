The average percentage of individuals residing in the U.S. who were not citizens at birth and were not citizens at the time of the population survey. The noncitizen population includes lawful permanent immigrants (or green-card holders), refugees and asylees, certain legal nonimmigrants (including those on student, work, or some other temporary visas), and persons residing in the country without authorization. See more detailed information [online](https://www.census.gov/programs-surveys/acs/technical-documentation/code-lists.2022.html#list-tab-155790978).
