The average percentage of registered “clunker” vehicles. Clunker vehicles, or vehicles 20 years or older, emit pollutants at higher levels because they typically lack advanced emission-control equipment. Data are from 2017 and reflect vehicles with model years from 1997 or earlier. See more detailed information [online](https://escholarship.org/uc/item/3mn6q0zm).
