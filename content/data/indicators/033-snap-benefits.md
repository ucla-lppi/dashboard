The percentage of individuals in a household receiving Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program (SNAP) benefits. SNAP, also known as food stamps, provides nutritional benefits to supplement the food budget of families in need. To be eligible for SNAP, a household’s income and assets must fall below a certain amount. See more detailed information about benefits and eligibility [online](https://www.cbpp.org/research/food-assistance/a-quick-guide-to-snap-eligibility-and-benefits).
